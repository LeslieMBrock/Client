Toilet Paper Rolls

One paper towel roll cut in half, gives you two pieces!

    Supplies Needed:

  • Colored Paper
  • Pipe Cleaners
  • Glue
  • Googly Eyes
  • Sharpie Marker

    Directions:

  1. Begin by cutting a piece of paper ( black or yellow) the same width as your paper towel roll.
  2. Wrap paper roll in paper, secure with glue or tape.
  3. Cut strips in the alternate color that you wrapped your body in.
  4. If you wrapped your roll in black, cut yellow strips. Glue or tape to toilet paper roll.
  5. Cut out a yellow head and two small black antennas.
  6. Create a face on the yellow head with googly eyes and a sharpie marker
  7. Attach antennas to the back of the yellow head.
  8. Add finished head piece to the top of your toilet paper roll.
  9. Use your toilet paper roll as a guide to draw and cut out wings.
  10. Tape or glue to the back of your toilet paper roll.
